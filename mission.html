🏛️ Core Pillars

What We Do

🤖
AI Bird Identification

Using Roboflow's computer vision models to enable anyone to identify bird species from a single photograph, making ornithology accessible to all.

🗺️
Geospatial Mapping

Building a living, real-time map of Philippine bird habitats and sightings — enabling conservationists to identify population trends and habitat threats.

👥
Citizen Science

Empowering ordinary Filipinos to become bird researchers. Every sighting report feeds into a growing scientific dataset used by conservation organizations.

🎮
Gamified Education

Making conservation engaging and addictive through a Pokédex-style collection system, XP rewards, and achievement badges that motivate continued exploration.

📊
Data-Driven Conservation

Aggregating community sightings into actionable biodiversity data that informs government policy, protected area management, and wildlife corridors.

🌿
Biodiversity Protection

Ultimately protecting the rich avian biodiversity of the Philippines — home to over 700 bird species, including 240+ endemic species found nowhere else on Earth.

🤝 Conservation Partners

Working Together for Birds

🦅
Haribon Foundation
The Philippines' pioneering bird conservation organization. Haribon has been protecting Philippine birds since 1972, conducting research, advocacy, and habitat protection programs across the archipelago.
Visit Haribon →
🦁
Philippine Eagle Foundation
Dedicated to the survival of the Philippine Eagle — the national bird and one of the world's most endangered raptors. The Foundation runs breeding programs, releases, and habitat protection in Mindanao.
Visit PEF →
🌿
WWF Philippines
World Wildlife Fund Philippines leads conservation efforts across critical biodiversity areas including the Palawan Biosphere Reserve, Tubbataha Reef, and key Philippine forest ecosystems.
Visit WWF PH →
🏛️
DENR (Dep't of Environment)
The Department of Environment and Natural Resources oversees protected areas, wildlife regulations, and environmental policy in the Philippines. A key government partner for data integration.
Visit DENR →
🔬
Bird Researchers Network
A network of academic researchers, ornithologists, and field biologists conducting scientific studies on Philippine bird populations, migration patterns, and biodiversity assessments.
📚
Educators & Birdwatchers Alliance
A coalition of teachers, nature educators, and birdwatching clubs across the Philippines who use our platform for environmental education and community science programs.

🔄 Impact Chain

From Sighting to Conservation

📸
Bird Sighting Observed

A citizen scientist spots a bird and uploads a photo to the platform using the AI identifier or manual sighting report.

🤖
AI Identification

Roboflow AI models analyze the photo to identify the species with confidence scores. The user is credited XP and the bird is added to their BirdDex.

🗺️
Geospatial Recording

GPS coordinates are captured and a sighting marker is added to the interactive habitat map, contributing to population distribution data.

📊
Data Aggregation

Sightings are aggregated into population trend data, habitat usage statistics, and regional biodiversity indices available to conservation partners.

🌿
Conservation Action

Conservation organizations use the data to prioritize habitat protection, inform government policy, and focus recovery efforts on threatened species.
